Who do we
work with?

Our background is deeply rooted in the energy sector, where we’ve spent years helping businesses define their value, strengthen their market position, and deliver commercially successful projects. That foundation means we work naturally with organisations operating in complex, technical environments where strategy, execution and commercial discipline all need to align.

But the same principles that drive success in energy apply across many other sectors. Over time, we’ve found our approach resonates just as strongly with businesses in manufacturing, technology, infrastructure and professional services – anywhere that a clear sales strategy, a strong go-to-market plan and the ability to execute effectively make the difference.

What our clients value is that we don’t just advise from a distance. We’ve built and led commercial teams ourselves, managed large pipelines, and delivered growth in fast-moving markets. That first-hand experience allows us to act as genuine partners – combining strategic insight with practical know-how to help businesses grow with confidence.

If your organisation is looking to sharpen its sales strategy or take a more structured approach to market growth, we’ll bring the same focus, energy and commercial mindset that have defined our work in the energy sector.
