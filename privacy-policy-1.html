1. Introduction

You can control or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings or our cookie banner.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, phone, or other device when you visit a website.
They help the site remember your preferences, recognise your device, and collect information about how you use the site.

There are two main types:

  • Session cookies – these are temporary and disappear when you close your browser.

  • Persistent cookies – these stay on your device until they expire or are deleted.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies – to make the site work properly (for example, enabling navigation or form submissions).

  • Analytics cookies – to understand how visitors use our website and help us improve content and usability (for example, through Google Analytics).

  • Functional cookies – to remember choices you make such as language or preferences.

  • Marketing or performance cookies – only if you’ve accepted them; these help us measure and improve how we promote our services.
